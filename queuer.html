queuer

QUEUER is a marketplace for the transaction
of money and time between people.

Sick of spending your precious time queueing for a seat at the famous Tendon Ginza Itsuki? With queuer, you can now find someone who has the spare time to queue for you!

All you have to do is to post a job listing, wait for the bids to flow in and pick the cheapest bid!


Technologies used: Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL.

Website: https://queuer.herokuapp.com/
Github: https://github.com/junkaiii/project2b
