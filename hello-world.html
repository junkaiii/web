Hello World

A proof of concept of a MMORPG done on PixiJS.

Having started with a game as my first project in General Assembly, I've decided to revisit my roots in my final project. Using Express and NodeJS, I created a server that allowed information to be exchanged within clients allowing multiplayer.
To play the game, press W for up, S for down, A for left and D for right.


Technologies used: NodeJS, Express, PixiJS, Socket.io.
