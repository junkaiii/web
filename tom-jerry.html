Tom & Jerry

A basic two-player cat and mouse game.

Everyone knows the drill in every Tom & Jerry episode. Tom chases Jerry, Jerry runs into the mousehole and Tom smashes into the wall. Well, that was what inspired me to create the game.

Players will either play Tom or Jerry in the game. The objective of the game is for Tom to catch Jerry. But heres the catch: Tom is twice as fast as Jerry, but Jerry can transverse through walls.


Technologies used: HTML5 Canvas, JQuery
