Jiak Simi

Hungry and strapped for time?
No problemo!

'Jiak Simi' (colloquial for what's for lunch?) is a Singapore-based food locator API created to help you locate food places that is within your preferred travelling distance.


Technologies used: NodeJS, MongoDB, Express.
Website: https://jiak-simi.herokuapp.com/
